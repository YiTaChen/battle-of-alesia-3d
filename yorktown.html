The Siege of Yorktown

SEP–OCT 1781 — VIRGINIA — WASHINGTON & ROCHAMBEAU vs. CORNWALLIS

Trapped on the York peninsula, Lord Cornwallis is sealed off by land by the American–French allied army and by sea by Admiral de Grasse’s French fleet. With no relief and no escape, the siege ends in the surrender that decides the American War of Independence.
