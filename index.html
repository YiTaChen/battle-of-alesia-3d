The Battle of Alesia

52 BCE — GAUL — CAESAR vs. VERCINGETORIX

Julius Caesar besieges the Gallic stronghold of Alesia — then is himself surrounded when a massive Gallic relief army arrives. The Romans must fight on two fronts at once, trapped between two enemy forces inside their own double ring of fortifications.
